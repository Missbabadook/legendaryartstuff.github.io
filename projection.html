Video Mapping Projection

Mushroom City, Mad Mapper, July 2022

This was a group exhibition between me and other peers that would not have been possible without our guest artist and instructor, Yannick Jacquet, and the Summer Arts program. My peers and I were divided up in four groups, where I collaborated with two other artist to compose the section of our city. We were divided into four groups, each group worked on four seperate sections of one whole.Our concept was city life and because there were four groups, our exhibition was named "Le Conte des Quatres Villes" which translates to the Tale of Four Cities. We agreed for the title to be in French as a tribute to Yannick. Our city represents connections, life, nature and fantasy. We instructed us on how to ideally align each surface and how to use the projection mapping software and two projectors, resulting in lighting up our cities to their highest potential. In my city, my group and crafted cardboard cutouts of various mushrooms and used other translucent materials to set up the environment. I used Adobe Photoshop to crop numerous mushroom stems and tops. My other peer used Adobe After Effects to create different sprites and animations. We brought our city to life when we fused all of these elements together through Mad Mapper to create the projection.
