No When, No Want, No Worries, Premiere Pro, 2020

This is a video that I made using Adobe Premiere Pro in art 75. The soundtrack was produced using Adobe Audition. I intended to primarily use jump cuts with subtle transitions. Through this video, I wanted to portray feelings of anxiety and isolation as it becomes a cycle. This artwork expresses anxiety and isolation during the pandemic as well as a transitional period of my life, and having to to adapt to a different way of life.
